Did You Know: Hip-Hop was born in The Bronx, New York in the early 70's thanks to DJ Kool Herc.

Grandmaster Flash and Afrika Bambaataa also paved the way and made hip-hop become wildly successful.

Although hip-hop in the 80's was golden, the 90's became very popular and artists such as 2pac, Nas, Wu-Tang Clan, The Notorious B.I.G and many others left an impact on the culture.
